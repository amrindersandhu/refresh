New Releases

Banner

Banners are another type of notification used to provide feedback contextually and prominently. They are persistent (but can be dismissed) and usually appear on top of a page or section.

→ Read Banner usage guidelines or Play with it in Storybook

 

BlankState

Blank states are used when a table, list, page, or section has no items or data to show. These states can be used as an opportunity to educate and guide users and help them progress in their tasks.

→ Read BlankState usage guidelines or Play with it in Storybook

 

Modal

Aww, the modal, a UI pattern that is not much loved. But we have built it because it is great for usability when used appropriately. And to help with appropriate usage we even created a small modal usage framework.

→ Read Modal usage guidelines or Play with it in Storybook

 

Search Input

This text input variant is specifically for search. It comes with a search icon and a clear button (appears when there is some input).

→ Play with it in Storybook

 

Toast

Toasts are a type of notification that temporarily appears on the bottom-right edge of the page. They help grab users’ attention to give feedback on their actions.

→ Read Toast usage guidelines or Play with it in Storybook

 

Truncation support in typography components

We have added support for truncation in for Heading and Text component. They support single-line and multi-line truncation.

→ Play with it in Storybook
